## Backup

Here you will find options to backup your server.

<Note> This backup option is an [Enterprise feature](./binary-backups). </Note>

![Ratel Backup](/images/ratel/ratel_backup.png)

### Creating a backup

Click on `Create Backup`. On the dialog box, you will choose the destination
details. After the successful backup it will be listed on the main panel.
